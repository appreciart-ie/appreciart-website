Questions

FAQs

Common questions about bookings, artists, and how the studio works. Can't find what you're looking for — get in touch.

01

What is Appreciart IE?

A private tattoo studio and gallery in Ballsbridge, Dublin. We work with resident and guest artists who treat tattooing as a fine art — not a service.

02

Who are the resident artists?

Moreirart, Marina Sadra and Renan Organicjoy. Each has a distinct approach — from abstract paletting to fluid art and organic blackwork. Their profiles are on the site — take a look before reaching out.

03

Do you host guest artists?

Yes, throughout the year. We bring in artists from outside Ireland for short residencies. Dates go up on the site and Instagram when they're set.

04

How do I book?

Through the Bookings page, or directly via WhatsApp. Tell us which artist you're interested in and what you have in mind — we'll take it from there.

05

Can I choose a specific artist?

Browse the portfolios and go with whoever feels right. If you're not sure, tell us what you have in mind and we'll point you in the right direction.

06

Do you offer consultations?

Yes. Most bookings start with a consultation — a chance to talk through the idea with the artist before anything is decided. A refundable deposit secures your slot. If no design is agreed at the consultation, the deposit comes back. If you cancel without 48 hours notice, it doesn't.

07

What exhibitions do you run?

The gallery space runs shows throughout the year — solo projects, collaborations, themed work. The focus is always on the work itself.

08

How do I stay updated?

Instagram is the fastest. We post artist arrivals, guest spots, flash days, and exhibition dates there first.

09

Can I bring my own design?

Yes. Bring references, sketches, or just a direction. The artists work with what you bring and develop something from there. You don't need a finished idea to start.
